About Me


I am a geologist who is making a career change to become a developer. I graduated from Brigham Young University in 2012 with my BS in Geology and 2014 with my MS in geology. My introduction to coding began in graduate school where I used python and matlab to process large data sets and run statistical analyses on several thousand data points. I am particuarly interested in data scicence, working with, manipulating, and analyzing data. As a professional, I have worked extensively on project teams, am capable of working independently, meeting deadlines, and communicating effectively with team members and clients.

I am excited to become a full stack developer and transition my career onto an exciting new path! When I am not coding, I love spending time with my family, watching sports, cooking, gardening, and keeping African Cichlids. I have two children and have been married nearly eight years.
